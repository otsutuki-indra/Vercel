Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 07, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HellCrop collects, uses, and protects your data. It applies to users in the United States and European Union (GDPR compliant).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, password, farm details
  • Crop & Usage Data: Growth metrics, soil data, harvest logs
  • Technical Data: IP address, device info, browser type
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via third-party providers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our crop growth platform
  • To personalize farming recommendations
  • To send important updates and support
  • To analyze platform performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared with trusted service providers only when necessary.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and regular security audits to protect your data.

5. Your Rights (GDPR for EU Users)

EU users have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict, or port their data. US users can exercise similar rights under CCPA.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for functionality and analytics cookies to improve user experience.
